Maranatha Ministry Center in the Hills of GALILEE - ISRAEL

DESCRIPTION

Maranatha Ministry Center is located in the High-Galilee mountains in Northern Israel. The facility is owned and operated by Israeli Christians who have lived in it for more than 40 years where they have hosted numerous of guests over the years to include travelers, evangelists, pastors, church groups and many more from all types of denominations and different countries. Long term lease options available. Please contact us for more information and available accommodation.
